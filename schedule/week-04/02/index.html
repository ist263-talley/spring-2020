Week04 – Homework

Style Your Resume

This week's homework will add styling to your HTML resume via cascading stylesheets (CSS).

Your process for doing this work will involve opening files from folder week-04/02/:

  • background.html
  • border.html
  • fonts.html
Refer to the HTML and the CSS that it calls to update your resume file from Week 03 Homework to add styling.

Guidelines

  • If you don't particularly like the gradient background, use it somewhere anyway. (The best place might be your header, but it's your choice.)
  • You can go to Google Fonts, if you like, to reference something other than the Roboto in the sample file.
  • Change the color, thickness, or other specs of the borders, but use them somewhere.

Thought for the Week's Class Summary

Why did you choose the styling you chose?
