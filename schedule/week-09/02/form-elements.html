HTML Forms & Elements

Allows us to submit information to the server

Basic Form Structure

method: Tells the browser HOW to send the information.

action: Tells the browser WHERE to send the information.

<form method="GET" action="/login.html">
   <!-- form elements go inside here -->
</form>

Example Login Form



<form method="GET" action="login.html">
    <p>
        <label>Email:</label><br>

    <p>

        <input type="password" name="login-pwd" value="" />
    </p>
    <p>
        <input type="submit" name="login" value="Login &raquo;" />
    </p>
</form>
